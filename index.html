Who We Are

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization based in Chhattisgarh. With a pan-India presence, we are committed to uplifting marginalized communities and fostering inclusive development.

Recognized by NITI Aayog and holding CSR-1, 80G, 12A, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certifications, our transparent operations ensure that every initiative reaches the right audience to create measurable social impact.

Our Key Initiatives

Humanitarian Aid

Project Seva

Serving humanity with compassion by distributing freshly prepared meals and clothing to daily wage workers and underprivileged communities.

Education

Project Bachpanshala

Nurturing young minds by ensuring quality education, free learning materials, and mentorship for children in low-income areas.

Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Empowering women towards a brighter future through skill development, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship training in rural India.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Extending compassion beyond humans by providing food, shelter, rescue, and medical treatment for stray and injured animals.

Environment

Project Prakriti

Advocating for sustainability through massive tree plantation drives, waste reduction campaigns, and eco-friendly community education.

Skill Development

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability by providing targeted skill development programs and internships for youth across various professional fields.
