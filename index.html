A Foundation of Hope

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization dedicated to fostering inclusive development across India. We believe every individual has the right to opportunities and basic necessities.

With strong credentials like 80G, 12A, CSR-1, and ISO certifications, we ensure transparency and measurable impact. From our roots in Chhattisgarh, we now operate nationwide, committed to lifting marginalized communities through compassion and collective action.

Join us on our mission to provide food, education, and empowerment, leaving no one behind.
